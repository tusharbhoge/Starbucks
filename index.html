The Starbucks Caffè Latte is a classic espresso beverage made with rich, full-bodied espresso shots and steamed milk, topped with a light layer of foam. It offers a creamy and smooth taste, combining the bold flavor of espresso with the natural sweetness of steamed milk.

The Strawberry Mocha at Starbucks is a delightful blend that combines rich mocha with sweet and tangy strawberry flavors. This beverage is often enjoyed both hot and iced, catering to different preferences and seasons.

Doppio espresso

₹225

A Doppio Espresso at Starbucks is essentially a double shot of espresso. "Doppio" means "double" in Italian, and it consists of two espresso shots, which provide a more intense and robust flavor compared to a single shot. The drink is known for its rich and bold taste, featuring a dense layer of crema on top

Matcha latte macchiato

₹350

The Matcha Latte Macchiato at Starbucks is a popular beverage that combines matcha green tea powder with milk, and it's often sweetened with syrup. This drink can be customized with different types of milk, including coconut milk, almond milk, and soy milk, as well as various syrups like vanilla, caramel, and hazelnut.
